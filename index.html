OneFish Ventures Investments

Working in close concert with management teams, we seek to reposition, rejuvenate, and grow businesses. With an intense focus on corporate strategy, operational excellence, and financial performance, we leverage our network, resources and experience to help build value for customers, employees, and shareholders.

We pursue investments with transaction values ranging from $2 million to over $20 million across a wide range of transaction types, customizing our approach as appropriate for each situation.
